前言 / Introduction

感谢您使用 EchoClockX（以下简称“本 App”）。我们非常重视您的隐私与数据安全。本隐私政策将说明我们如何处理您的数据。本 App 遵守苹果 App Store 审核指南和相关法律法规。

Thank you for using this application (“the App”). We value your privacy and data security. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data. The App complies with Apple’s App Store Review Guidelines and applicable laws.

1. 我们收集的信息 / Information We Collect

本 App 不会主动收集、存储、上传或共享任何个人信息。所有数据均仅在您的设备本地处理，不会发送至开发者或任何第三方。

The App does NOT collect, store, upload, or share any personal information. All data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to the developer or any third parties.

为实现功能，App 可能需要以下系统权限： / To enable certain features, the App may require the following permissions:

1.1 麦克风权限 / Microphone Access

用于录制音频、制作铃声。录制文件仅保存在您的设备上。

Used for recording audio and creating ringtones. Recorded files are stored only on your device.

1.2 文件访问权限 / File Access

用于导入音频文件或保存文件至本地目录。所有操作均在本地完成。

Used to import or save audio files. All file operations occur locally on your device.

1.3 通知权限（如适用）/ Notification Permission (if applicable)

用于提醒功能。App 不会收集任何通知内容。

Used for reminder features. The App does not collect or process notification content.

本 App 不会收集或上传以下任何信息：

The App does NOT collect or upload any of the following:

  • 姓名 / Name

  • 位置数据 / Location data
  • 设备标识符（如 IDFA）/ Device identifiers (e.g., IDFA)
  • 使用行为或分析统计数据 / Usage analytics
  • 音频/录音内容 / Audio or recording content

所有内容均保存在您的设备本地。Everything stays local on your device.

The App provides a “Lifetime VIP” in-app purchase. All payments are processed by Apple. The developer cannot access any of your payment information.

用户可通过 Apple 提供的 恢复购买（Restore Purchases） 功能恢复已购项目。

You may restore previous purchases using Apple’s Restore Purchases feature.

The App does not use any third-party SDKs, advertising services, or analytics tools.

所有数据仅存储在您的设备本地，由系统沙盒保护。删除 App 后，所有本地数据将一并被清除。

All data is stored locally and protected by the system sandbox. Deleting the App removes all associated local data.
